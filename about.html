About

Hello! I'm a student at the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire, pursuing my passion for graphic communications with the dream of becoming a graphic designer. Originally from Pewaukee, Wisconsin, I've always had a creative spirit and an eye for aesthetics. My journey into the world of design has been driven by my love for art, photography, and a deep appreciation for the power of visual communication. Studying graphic communications allows me to explore the intricate blend of creativity, technology, and storytelling. I am dedicated to honing my skills and embracing the ever-evolving digital landscape to create meaningful and impactful designs. My love for photography adds an extra dimension to my artistic toolbox, enabling me to capture moments and perspectives that inspire my work. Outside of my academic pursuits, I am an avid traveler, always seeking new adventures and cultural experiences. If you share a love for design, photography, or travel, let's connect and share our experiences and insights!
